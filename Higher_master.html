· IDLE — coral shadow : the badge breathes. Calm presence. Subliminal neuro signal that the button is alive. (ALPHA · awareness)
· HOVER — white shadow : the badge sharpens. Attention is rising. The user has engaged consciously. (BETA renewed · intent)
